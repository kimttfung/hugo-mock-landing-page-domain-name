Destination Voting

A feature that enables group members to democratically vote on proposed destinations, ensuring that the final choice reflects the group's collective preference.

Group Expense Tracking

A tool for logging, categorizing, and tracking all trip-related expenses in real time, facilitating transparent and fair budget management among group members.

Personalized Activities

An AI-based system that suggests experiences tailored to the individual interests, enhancing the overall trip enjoyment with personalized activities.

Availability Coordinator

A shared calendar feature that allows group members to mark their available dates for travel, simplifying the process of finding a mutually convenient time for the trip.

Access On the Go

Provides travelers with real-time access to the trip's finalized itinerary from their mobile devices, keeping everyone informed and prepared while on the go.
